The Design System
A quiet, paper-warm system for a place to think. Serif for the thinking, a clean grotesk for the doing, and one ember of orange for the spark of an idea.
01 Two ways to think
Spark
A single note. Just write — a thought caught before it escapes. Always marked by the ember flame.
Cluster
A space to grow ideas. Notes drawn together into a living map of connections. Marked by the constellation.
